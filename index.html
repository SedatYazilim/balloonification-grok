About Grok

What is Grok?

Grok is a generative AI chatbot developed by xAI, Elon Musk's artificial intelligence company. Launched in November 2023, Grok is designed to be a truth-seeking AI companion with unfiltered answers, real-time access to data from X (formerly Twitter), and frontier capabilities in reasoning, coding, and visual processing.

The Evolution of Grok

From a bold experiment to the world's most capable AI — the Grok journey in four milestones.

Origin
Grok 1
November 2023
The first Grok. Launched exclusively for X Premium+ subscribers. Introduced real-time X integration and an unfiltered, witty personality inspired by The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy.
Expansion
Grok 2
August 2024
Major upgrade with image understanding and generation. Introduced Aurora image model, expanded to more users, and added enterprise-grade document analysis capabilities.
Reasoning
Grok 3
February 2025
Trained on xAI's Colossus supercluster with 10x the compute of Grok 2. Introduced DeepSearch, Big Brain mode, and advanced reasoning. Outperformed GPT-4o on key benchmarks.
Latest
Grok 4
July 2025
The world's smartest AI according to xAI. Available via SuperGrok subscription. Grok 4 Heavy offers multi-agent reasoning for the most challenging tasks. $20B Series E funded.
